BLACK WEALTH CONNECT WOMEN'S SUMMIT 2025

Following high demand and an explosive launch in 2024, the Black Wealth Connect Women’s Summit returns for its second year on 8 November at the Footscray Community Arts Centre in Melbourne. The event will bring together over 150 women for a full day of connection, learning, and empowerment, running from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

This year’s summit will feature three impactful keynote speakers offering valuable insights across finance, entrepreneurship, and mindset. While the official theme will be announced soon, the day promises powerful conversations, practical tools, and uplifting energy for women at every stage of their journey.

A standout addition for 2025 is the introduction of tech in business—a new focus exploring how women can use digital tools and innovation to grow their ventures and careers.

The summit will also celebrate creativity and community, with live performances, art displays, and a vibrant marketplace of vendor stalls showcasing exceptional women-led businesses and creatives.

Engaging panel discussions will cover a wide range of relevant topics in business, creativity, leadership, and finance. These themes and speakers will be announced in the coming weeks, promising a diverse and powerful lineup.

As always, financial literacy remains at the heart of Black Wealth Connect. Whether attendees are navigating creative industries, launching a startup, or exploring tech, they’ll leave with fresh insights, meaningful connections, and the tools to build long-term success.

The Black Wealth Connect Women’s Summit 2025 is set to be a vibrant and empowering experience—championing the brilliance, ambition, and leadership of Black women and women of colour making bold moves and building legacy.
