BLACK WEALTH CONNECT PODCAST SEASON 3

The Black Wealth Connect Podcast returns for its powerful third season, launching with none other than Dr. Umar Johnson as the first guest. Known for his unapologetic voice on education and Black empowerment, Dr. Johnson sets the tone for a season filled with depth, insight, and impact.

Hosted by Fabrice Omankoy, founder of Black Wealth, the podcast continues its mission to spark honest conversations around financial literacy, mindset, and entrepreneurship—especially within the Black and African diaspora. After two successful seasons, Season 3 will feature 10 new episodes, each diving into the realities of building wealth, shifting narratives, and creating generational change.

This season will welcome some of the most forward-thinking minds across business, finance, tech, and creative industries, offering listeners practical tools and powerful stories that inspire action.

New episodes drop monthly on all major streaming platforms. Subscribe, tune in, and grow—this season is about ownership, elevation, and legacy.
