Powered by Black Excellence

Black Wealth Connect is an Australian-based platform dedicated to elevating the current and next generation of African-Australian professionals by uniting the community, bridging knowledge gaps, and empowering individuals with the tools, resources, and representation needed to thrive. With a mission deeply rooted in celebrating Black excellence and driving collective growth, we focus on financial literacy and education, leadership and mindset, creative arts and athletics, entrepreneurship, and philanthropy.

We host the annual Black Wealth Connect Summit and the Black Wealth Connect Womenâ€™s Summit, creating spaces for changemakers, thought leaders, and innovators to connect, collaborate, and inspire. These events have become cornerstones for building networks, amplifying voices, fostering leadership, and nurturing both personal and professional success within the African-Australian community.

Our platform also extends into media and digital spaces. The Black Wealth Connect Podcast delivers monthly episodes featuring real stories, expert insights, and practical knowledge across our core focus areas. Through BWC Digital, we offer a membership program designed to provide on-demand videos, live monthly webinars, practical resources, and opportunities to connect and grow with peers. This initiative also focuses on empowering youth to build leadership skills and pursue excellence from an early age.

With a growing audience of over 12,000 across social platforms and 600+ active email subscribers, alongside an extended reach of 100,000+ through our network of speakers and partners, Black Wealth Connect continues to inspire, educate, and uplift African-Australian communities while cultivating a vision for global impact.
