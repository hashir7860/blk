BLACK WEALTH CONNECT SUMMIT 2025

The Black Wealth Connect Summit took place on June 28th in Sydney and welcomed over 150 incredible people including creatives, entrepreneurs, business owners, youth, professionals, and individuals from all walks of life. Now in its third year, the summit has become a space where community, collaboration, and growth come alive, reminding us of how much we can achieve when we come together to share knowledge and uplift each other.

Throughout the day, meaningful connections were made and powerful conversations took place about financial literacy and its importance across all industries, mental wellbeing and mindset, and the value of building generational wealth. The program featured three keynote speakers who shared inspiring insights, three dynamic panel discussions that explored diversifying income, leadership, and navigating the creative industry, as well as interactive workshops on tax strategies, leadership skills, mindset, and personal branding. Attendees left with practical tools and a renewed sense of confidence to take action in their personal and professional lives.

The energy in the room was unforgettable, filled with collaboration, learning, and cultural celebration, including an incredible live performance that brought everyone together in such a powerful way. The summit proved once again to be an impactful and inspiring experience that highlighted the importance of creating spaces where our community can connect, grow, and thrive.

We’re already looking forward to continuing this journey and can’t wait for the 4th Annual Black Wealth Connect Summit in 2026.
